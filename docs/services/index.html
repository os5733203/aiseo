What is the difference between Technical SEO and Content Strategy? +
Technical SEO fixes the infrastructure of your website — crawlability, indexing, page speed, Core Web Vitals and site architecture. Content Strategy creates and publishes SEO-optimised content targeting specific keywords to build topical authority. Both services work together: technical SEO ensures Google can find and index your pages, while content strategy gives Google the right pages to rank.
