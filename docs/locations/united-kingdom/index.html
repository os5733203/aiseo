Understanding the UK SEO Landscape in 2026

The United Kingdom is one of the most competitive English-language SEO markets in the world. Google holds approximately 92% of UK search traffic. The UK digital advertising market was valued at over $30 billion in 2025, making it one of the largest digital economies in Europe.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation

What Is GEO?

GEO is the practice of structuring website content so it is cited by AI systems including Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. AI Overviews appear for 68% of UK local searches. Businesses with vague websites or inconsistent business details are increasingly invisible in AI-generated answers.

AEO — Answer Engine Optimisation

What Is AEO?

AEO focuses on formatting content so AI systems can extract clear, citable answers — 40 to 60 word direct-answer snippets, numbered lists, comparison tables, and FAQ schema for specific local questions like 'How much does an electrician cost in Manchester?'

Why AI Visibility Requires Good SEO Foundations

The UK AI Search Reality

AI visibility requires the same foundations as strong local SEO: a complete GBP, consistent NAP, genuine reviews, authoritative backlinks, and well-structured content. AI search is an amplifier of existing SEO quality — not a separate channel requiring separate tactics.
