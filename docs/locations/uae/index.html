GEO · AEO · 2026

From Ranking to Being Selected as the Answer

The objective for UAE businesses has shifted. GEO and AEO are not optional extras in 2026 — they are the mechanisms that determine whether AI systems surface your business or your competitor's.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation

What Is GEO?

GEO is the practice of structuring website content in both Arabic and English so it is cited, quoted, or surfaced by AI systems including Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. In the UAE, AI Overviews appear on the majority of commercial searches.

AEO — Answer Engine Optimisation

What Is AEO?

AEO focuses on formatting content so AI systems can extract clear, citable answers — 40 to 60 word definitions in Arabic and English, numbered lists, and comparison tables that Google can extract as featured snippets and AI systems can cite.

WhatsApp as a Conversion Signal

The UAE's Primary Conversion Channel

In the UAE, WhatsApp is the primary way high-intent local customers contact businesses. Every UAE local SEO strategy must include prominent WhatsApp CTAs on mobile views, WhatsApp Business integration, and click-to-WhatsApp tracking as a primary conversion metric.

Hyper-Local Emirate Pages

One Page Per Emirate — Never Bundle

Build one dedicated, hyper-optimised hub page per target Emirate or free zone. Never bundle all services onto one page. One specific user intent = one dedicated landing page — treating Dubai and Abu Dhabi as the same market consistently underperforms.
